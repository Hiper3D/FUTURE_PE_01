## 2. CORE SERVICES PAGE

**Service 1: Comprehensive Full Body Checkup**
Preventive healthcare is your first line of defense. Our comprehensive full-body profiles are designed to detect silent health issues before they become emergencies, giving you total peace of mind.
* **What’s Included:**
  * Complete Blood Count (CBC) and Lipid Profile.
  * Liver Function (LFT) and Kidney Function (KFT) tests.
  * Thyroid screening and fasting blood sugar levels.
* **Why Choose Us:** We offer affordable, transparently priced packages with a free post-test consultation to help you understand your reports.

**Service 2: Advanced Pathology & Blood Profiling**
From routine fever panels to complex hormonal assays, our core pathology lab handles all diagnostics with uncompromising precision. 
* **What’s Included:**
  * Dengue, Malaria, and Typhoid rapid screening.
  * Vitamin B12 and Vitamin D3 deficiency tracking.
  * HbA1c testing for diabetes management.
* **Why Choose Us:** Our fully automated hematology lab eliminates human error, ensuring your doctor gets the exact data they need to treat you effectively.

**Service 3: Express At-Home Blood Collection**
Don't let a busy schedule or limited mobility stop you from prioritizing your health. Our certified technicians bring the diagnostic lab directly to your living room.
* **What’s Included:**
  * Safe, hygienic sample collection using single-use, sterile equipment.
  * Flexible booking slots that fit your daily schedule.
  * Digital reports delivered securely via WhatsApp and Email by evening.
* **Why Choose Us:** Our phlebotomists are specially trained in painless vein detection, making the process smooth and stress-free, even for elderly patients and children.
