Act as a Senior Conversion Copywriter for a digital marketing agency. Your objective is to generate a complete, conversion-focused website copy wireframe for a local business. 

SYSTEM VARIABLES:
- Business Name: Bhubaneswar CityCare Diagnostics
- Business Type: Diagnostic Center & Pathology Lab
- Location: Bhubaneswar, Odisha
- Target Audience: Families, elderly patients, and busy professionals needing fast, accurate health tests.
- Tone Adaptation: Professional, highly empathetic, authoritative, and reassuring. (Avoid aggressive sales language; focus on trust and accuracy).

DELIVERABLES & FORMATTING:
Generate the copy using the following structured sections. Do not use generic AI filler like "Welcome to our website."

1. HOMEPAGE COPY
- Primary Headline (H1): A powerful, benefit-driven value proposition (max 8 words).
- Sub-headline (H2): Clarify exactly who you help and how (max 2 sentences).
- Trust-Building Intro: A 3-4 sentence paragraph explaining your local roots in Bhubaneswar, your commitment to accuracy, and why patients choose you over corporate hospital labs.

2. CORE SERVICES PAGE
List 3 distinct services. For each service, provide:
- Service Title
- A 2-sentence persuasive description.
- 3 bullet points detailing exactly "What's Included."
- A 1-sentence "Why Choose Us" differentiator for this specific service.

3. CALL-TO-ACTION (CTA) FRAMEWORKS
Write 3 distinct CTA blocks that can be placed across the site:
- Direct Booking CTA: For the top navigation bar or hero section.
- Trust/Authority CTA: Focused on certifications (NABL/ISO) and report accuracy.
- Urgency/Convenience CTA: Focused on same-day home collection in Bhubaneswar.
